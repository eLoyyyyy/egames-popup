The object of the game is to predict where the ball will come to rest on the Roulette wheel. The Roulette wheel consists of numbers 1 through 12, alternately colored red and black, plus 0, which is cadet blue.

To play the game:

  • Click on a chip of the desired value to select it. Then click on any bet area on the table to place your bet there. Every click on the area adds one chip of the selected value to the bet. Select another chip if you wish to increase the bet by a different amount. You can place several chips into several different bet areas at the same time.

  • Shift clicking on the area removes one chip of the same value as the selected chip, or the entire bet if the selected chip is greater in value than the bet placed. Clicking Clear Bets removes all the placed bets from the table.

  • The minimum and maximum bet limits are dependent on your VIP level. Bets that are not within the limits appear as transparent on the table. For detailed information on the bet limits, hover or click on the limits sign on the table.

  • Click Spin to set the wheel spinning.

Notes:

All different betting options and corresponding payouts are explained in the Mini Roulette Rules section.

Buttons:

Clear Bets
Clear the table of previous bets.
Rebet
Place the same bets as in your previous round.
Spin
Spin the wheel.

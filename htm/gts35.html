Medusa's Gaze is an online four-reel soft slot game. The aim is to achieve a winning combination of tiles on any of the 12 win lines.

  • Your stake is altered by clicking on the increment buttons, or on the max stake button.
  • When you are happy with your selection, click the play button to start.

Win Lines

Win by matching 3 or more consecutive tiles anywhere on one of the 12 win lines.

Cascades

After every winning spin, Medusa turns the winning tiles to stone. The remaining tiles then cascade down towards the centre to fill the empty spaces.

Multiplier

After every cascade, the multiplier increments by 1, increasing the payouts of any wins formed as a result of the cascade.

Wild

Wild can substitute for any tile. Wild does not pay by itself.

Auto Play

There is an auto play function available that will start each spin automatically until either something occurs that requires your intervention or the number of set spins expires. Select your desired number of spins using the increase and decrease buttons. Click ‘Stop’ to cancel auto play.

Maximum Win Limit

The maximum win in the game has an upper limit. For more information, see the Terms and Conditions section.

Return to Player

The theoretical percentage return to player (RTP) is 96.60%.

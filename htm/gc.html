3-Reel 3-Row Multispin Slots

The object of Goblin's Cave is to obtain a winning symbol combination by spinning the reels.

To play the game:

  • Choose the value of the coins you wish to wager each round, by clicking on the + and – buttons on the left of the screen.
  • Click Bet One or Max Bet to select the number of coins you wish to wager. Each click on the Bet One button wagers one more coin. Pressing Max Bet increases your bet amount to the maximum amount possible – that is five coins– and spins the reels automatically. Remember that your total bet is three times the bet on one row. You can see your remaining credit, the bet amounts and win amount on the machine left of the reels.
  • Click Spin (if you did not press Bet Max) to spin the reels.
  • After the reels stop, select the reels you want to hold, by clicking on the Hold buttons beneath them or on the reels themselves. Click on Spin again to spin the reels you did not hold.
  • If any of the rows show a winning combination after they stop, you win according to the winning table. Click the Paytablebutton to see the paytable, if you wish. Flip the pages with Next/Back, and close the paytable with Close. You can also click the Paytable button again to close the paytable.

About the winning tables:

The tables show a winning amount for every symbol combination that deserves a win. The payouts shown in the paytable are multiplied by the line bet. If you have multiple wins on one line (a rare, but possible event), then only the biggest win is accounted for. If you have winning combinations on multiple lines, they are all added up.

The red diamond with the word “Wild” written on it, is a wild symbol – meaning it stands for any other symbol, like a joker in card games.

Please note: There is a difference between the line bet and the total bet. The line bet shows how much you're betting on a single payline. The total bet shows how much you're spending in this game round. The payouts shown in the paytable are multiplied by the line bet, not by the total bet. If you get winning combinations on more than one payline, the winnings for each payline are added up.

Bonus Game:

Goblin's Cave contains a Bonus Game. To access the bonus game, you have to collect three lantern symbols on one row. If you get three lanterns on the first row and another three on the second, you get to play two bonus rounds, and the same goes for three. In the bonus game, choose one of the chests by clicking on it. It contains a prize for you.

Buttons:

Click this to see the winnings table. Click it again to close it.

Bet One

Bet one to five coins (click one to five times). Click Spin to spin the reels after that.

Max Bet

Bet all five coins with one click and spin the reels automatically.

Spin

Spin the reels.

Hold

Hold the reels you want, after the first spin.

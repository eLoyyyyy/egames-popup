Wheel of Light is a roulette-style game. The aim is to correctly predict the number that the pointer will stop on at the end of a spin. Bets range from predicting the exact number, a spread of numbers or whether the number will be odd or even.

BET TYPES

There are a wide range of bet types available:

  • Exact number bet: one of 31 numbers (0-30)

  • Colour bet: one of 4 coloured ranges

  • Number spread bet: 1-5, 6-13, 14-16, 17-23 or 24-30

  • Hi/Lo bet: Hi (16-30) or Lo (1-15)

  • Even/Odd bet: Even number or odd number

Please note: you can only win on 0 by placing an exact number bet on it. It is not included in any other bet type.

SELECTING A CHIP VALUE

Select a chip by clicking on the chip of your choice. The value of the chip is indicated.

PLACING A BET

To place a bet, click on one of the bet placement areas on the wheel. The chip value will be added to the stake for that bet. Click repeatedly to increase your stake by the current chip value. As your mouse moves over a betting area, details of that bet, the odds and any stakes on it will be shown. You may place as many bets as you wish on a single spin. Your total stake is always updated and displayed.

REMOVING A CHIP

  • To cancel individual bets, hold down the shift key and click on the bet you wish to remove. All chips will be removed from this bet.

  • Click 'Clear All' to remove all chips from the wheel and cancel all bets placed.

SPINNING THE WHEEL

Click the bet button to submit your bets and spin the wheel.

WINNINGS

At the end of a game, a win message will confirm any winnings.

5-reel 25-line slots

The objective of Spider-Man: Attack of the Green Goblin is to obtain winning symbol combinations by spinning the reels.

To play the game:

  • Line bets are chosen by clicking + and – below Line Bet to increase or decrease the amount.
  • The Spider-Man: Attack of the Green Goblin game is always played with 25 active paylines. The number of active paylines is fixed and cannot be changed by the player.
  • Total bet per game round = line bet X active paylines.
  • Clicking Spin spins the reels with the current selection of lines and line bets. During the reel spins the Spin button changes intoStop. Clicking Stop ends the spin animation and immediately displays the spin results.
  • Reels can also be spun using the Auto Play function. Mouse over the Auto Play button to display the list of options. Select the number of spins to be played automatically or choose Until Feature to spin until the Spider-Man Collection Feature, Spider-Man Wild Feature, or Spidey Snapshot Feature is triggered. Clicking on an option starts the Auto Play function. The Auto Playbutton changes into Stop during the Auto Play mode. The Auto Play mode ends when the reels have been spun the number of times determined by the player, or when the Spider-Man Collection Feature, Spider-Man Wild Feature, or Spidey Snapshot Feature is triggered if you chose Until Feature, or when you click Stop.
  • Clicking the Turbo Mode button switches on or off the Turbo Mode thus turning on or off some of the win animations and sounds, and making the reels spin faster or slower.
  • Wins are calculated according to the paytable. Line win = line bet X corresponding multiplier according to the paytable. The paytable can be accessed via the Info page.
  • On a given payline, only the highest payline winning combination pays while simultaneous winnings on different paylines are accumulated.
  • In case of a winning spin, the Win field displays the accumulating winnings. The Win ticker can be stopped by clicking anywhere on the screen to display the total win amount immediately.
  • Payline wins and the total win are also displayed on the strip located at the bottom of the reels or the game window.

Info page:

  • Clicking Info opens the reference screen describing different game components. Clicking the arrow buttons at the lower right-hand corner of the screen enables navigating between the different info screens.
    • The Paytable screen shows all winning combinations. When opened after a winning spin, the winning symbol combinations (number of symbols and bet multiplier) are highlighted and blink.
    • The Spider-Man Wild Feature screen explains how the feature is triggered and describes its flow while active.
    • The Spidey Snapshot Feature screen describes how the feature can be triggered and explains its rules while active.
    • The Spider-Man Collection screen explains the conditions for the bonus round to start off and describes its prizes.
    • The Ultimate Fight Feature screen explains when the feature begins and what its rules are.
    • The City Chase Feature screen shows when the feature is triggered and explains its rules.
    • The Hot Zone Free Games screen describes how the Hot Zone Free Games are triggered and played.
    • The Rivaling Free Games screen indicates how the Rivaling Feature is triggered and explains the Rivaling Free Games rules.
    • The Radioactive Free Games screen shows how the Radioactive Spider Feature is triggered and explains the Radioactive Free Games rules.
    • The Marvel Multi-Level Mystery Progressive Jackpot screen describes how to win entry to the Marvel Multi-Level Mystery Progressive Jackpot game that wins one of four jackpots, and explains the game’s rules.
    • The Paylines screen illustrates all possible payline combinations and explains the payout rules in the game.
  • Clicking Back exits the Info screen and returns to the game.

Paylines:

  • Active paylines are represented by lines that appear over the reels as illustrated on the Paylines screen of the Info page.
  • All 25 active paylines can register wins.
  • There is a difference between the line bet and the total bet. The line bet shows how much is being bet on a single payline. The total bet shows how much is being bet in total on the game round. Payouts shown in the paytable are multiplied by the line bet.

About payouts:

  • Payouts are listed on the Paytable screen. To find the possible win amount, the line bet must be multiplied by the payout.
  • If two payline winning combinations occur on the same line, the higher of them is paid out. If more than one active payline has a winning combination, the winnings are accumulated.
  • Winning combinations must start from the left most reel, and the symbols have to be consecutive.

Ultimate Fight Feature

On the screen that appears Spider-Man and the Green Goblin face each other getting ready for a battle. 5 are revealed before every strike takes place. Clicking on any of these will determine both who will hit the next strike and how many power units will be taken in case of a successful strike, or if power will be restored to Spider-Man. Both participants will start the fight with a full power bar of 20 units. If you pick the power restoring option, Spider-Man will always receive 6 power units back. The fight will end when either Spider-Man or the Green Goblin runs out of their power bar units.

Whenever Spider-Man strikes successfully, the Green Goblin will lose the same number of power units as the number written on that has been picked by you. If Spider-Man strikes successfully, the hit is awarded a cash prize of the total bet x2 for every power unit reduced from the Green Goblin’s power bar. If Spider-Man strikes successfully 3 consecutive times, a Super Combo strike in his favour will follow. The Green Goblin will then lose a larger amount of power and you are awarded a cash prize equal to the total bet x3 for every power unit reduced from the Green Goblin’s power bar. The more powerful the Super Combo strike, the higher the cash reward. If Spider-Man has won the fight, you will be awarded an additional cash prize equal to the total bet x2 for each power unit left for Spider-Man after the fight has ended. If Spider-Man wins the fight with all 20 power units, your entire bonus wins will be multiplied x5. If after the fight his power units are 17, 18, or 19, your entire bonus wins will be multiplied x2.

Whenever the Green Goblin strikes successfully, Spider-Man will lose the same number of power units as the number written on that has been picked by you. No cash prize is awarded for any successful strike of the Green Goblin. If the Green Goblin strikes successfully 3 consecutive times, a Super Combo strike in his favour will follow. Spider-Man will then lose a larger amount of power, but you will not be awarded any cash prize after the Combo strike has ended. If the Green Goblin has won the fight, your bonus win will be the cash prize awarded for the successful strikes of Spider-Man.

Note: The minimum amount of power units cannot be negative. For example: if during the fight the Green Goblin has been left with 1 power unit and Spider-Man strikes after you have picked a revealing 3, the Green Goblin will lose the fight ending with 0 power units.

After the Ultimate Fight Feature has ended, a result board summarizes the winnings. Game Win shows the winnings that were received from the main game spin that won the Spider-Man Collection Bonus. Feature Win shows the winnings that were received during the Ultimate Fight Feature. Total Win shows the accumulated winnings (Game Win and Feature Win added up).

During the chase each time Spider-Man swings from place to place he might (or might not) collect Hazard Spidey Sense or Intuition Spidey Sense.

The Hazard Spidey Sense helps the player evade the Collect option and can pick again. Once Hazard Spidey Sense has been used it cannot be used again but another Spidey sense can be collected along the way. If you pick Collect again, then the City Chase Feature ends.

The Intuition Spidey Sense allows you to see the prizes behind the items that appear during the mini-bonus following its collection. The highest cash prize is always highlighted.

Note: You can have only 1 Hazard Spidey Sense at a time. Only one Hazard Spidey Sense can be collected at a time. However, Intuition Spidey Sense can still be collected.

Hot Zone Free Games

You are awarded 20 Hot Zone Free Games. While the reels spin Spider-Man can appear swinging across the screen and throw cobwebs at the reels thus creating “Hot Zones” of 2x2 positions each time he shows up. If during the next 3 spins, or are caught in the Hot Zone, they remain “stuck” until the “hot zone” ends.

“Hot zones” can overlap if Spider-Man throws a cobweb before the existing “hot zone” has expired. In this case the “stuck” symbols on the overlapping positions between the different “hot zones”, if there are any, will remain there until the latest “hot zone” ends.

Note: Whenever gets “stuck” it becomes Extra Wild. If a winning combination occurs along an active payline with as “stuck” standing in, the payout is multiplied x2. Each “stuck” multiplies the result only once for one and the same active payline. However, if 2 or more “stuck” appear in a winning combination, the active payline win will be multiplied x2. 3 or more “stuck” acquire the payment behavior of the regular Wild except in the cases when the result has to be multiplied x2. Goblin appearing behind a stuck Wild will replace the Wild and now be stuck in the “Hot Zone”.

The Hot Zone Free Games cannot be retriggered.

Marvel Mystery Jackpot

Marvel Mystery Jackpot is a multi-level progressive jackpot game that is available in all Marvel games and is linked to all Marvel games. A small percentage of each bet in the Marvel games, by every person playing this game in each of many online casinos, is added to the progressive jackpot.

The trigger that starts the Jackpot game is random and can occur after any spin in any of the linked games. It is not caused by any symbol combination in the linked games and even a spin that does not win anything in the main game can trigger the Jackpot game. Likewise, any bet with any size in the linked games can trigger the Jackpot game. Nonetheless, the chances of triggering the Jackpot game increase or decrease proportionally to the bet size. Entering the Jackpot game guarantees a win of one of the four jackpots. Playing the Jackpot game reveals the jackpot that has been awarded to you, but does not determine it.

There are four different levels of jackpots that you can win: Power Jackpot, Extra Power Jackpot, Super Power Jackpot and Ultimate Power Jackpot. Different types of jackpots hold different prize amounts. The jackpot gamescreen contains 20 squares which in the beginning of the gameplay are turned upside down. Clicking on a square will turn it around and reveal one of four jackpot symbols on the other side. Once you have found three matching symbols, you win the jackpot that corresponds to these symbols. Jackpot winnings are added to the main game winnings (if any) and are displayed in the Win field together with any other winnings.

The Marvel Mystery Jackpot game has a timeout timer. This means that if you do not pick a square in a set amount of time, you will automatically receive your prize.

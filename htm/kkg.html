The objective of Kong The Eighth Wonder of The World is to obtain winning symbol combinations by spinning the reels.

To play the game:

  • Line bets are chosen by clicking + and – below Line Bet to increase or decrease the amount.
  • Paylines are chosen by clicking + or - below Lines. Paylines can also be activated by using the numbered buttons on either side of the reels. Selecting a high payline includes all the lower ones too. For example, selecting payline 6 also activates paylines 1 through 5. Clicking Bet Max activates all paylines with the currently selected bet per line and spins the reels.
  • Total bet per game round = line bet X active paylines.
  • Clicking Spin spins the reels with the current selection of lines and line bets. During the reel spins the Spin button changes intoStop. Clicking Stop ends the spin animation and immediately displays the spin results.
  • Reels can also be spun using the Auto Play function.
    Roll over the Auto Play button to display the options: 10, 20, 30 or 50 spins. Clicking on any of them selects the successive number of spins to be enabled and spins the reels. The Auto Play button changes into Stop during the Auto Play mode. The Auto Play mode ends when the reels have been spun the number of times determined by the player, or when you click Stop.
  • Wins are calculated according to the paytable. Line win = line bet X corresponding multiplier according to the paytable. Scatter win = total bet X corresponding multiplier according to the paytable. The paytable can be accessed via the Info page. When opened after a winning spin, the winning symbol combinations (number of symbols and bet multiplier) are highlighted and blink.
  • On a given payline, only the highest payline winning combination pays while simultaneous winnings on different paylines are accumulated.
  • In case of a winning spin, the Win field displays the accumulated winnings. The Win ticker can be stopped by clicking anywhere on the screen to display the total win amount immediately.
  • Payline wins and the total win are also displayed on the strip located at the bottom of the reels or the game window.

Paylines:

  • Active paylines are represented by lines that appear over the reels. Paylines can be activated and their shape displayed by progressively clicking + and – below Lines.
  • Only active paylines can register wins.
  • There is a difference between the line bet and the total bet. The line bet shows how much is being bet on a single payline. The total bet shows how much is being bet in total on the game round. Payouts shown in the paytable are multiplied by the line bet.

The Scatter symbol is an exception to these rules. More information about the Scatter symbol can be found below.

About payouts:

  • Payouts are listed on the Paytable screen. To find the possible win amount, the line bet must be multiplied by the payout.
  • If two payline winning combinations occur on the same line, the higher of them is paid out. If more than one active payline has a winning combination, the winnings are accumulated.
  • Winning combinations must start from the left most reel, and the symbols have to be consecutive.

The Scatter symbol is an exception to these rules. More information about the Scatter symbol can be found below.

Jungle Mode and Big City Mode

The game Kong The Eighth Wonder of The World is played in two modes: Jungle Mode and Big City Mode. In each of the two modes, the symbols change visually but the paytable remains the same. In Jungle Mode, the Wild Jungle Respins feature and the Skull Island Bonus can be triggered. In Big City Mode, the Wild Kong Respins feature and the City Tower Bonus can be triggered. Exiting Skull Island Bonus begins the Big City Mode, while exiting City Tower Bonus begins Jungle Mode.

By default, the game begins in Jungle Mode.

Wild Symbol

The Wild symbol in the game is the symbol in Jungle Mode and in Big City Mode. The symbols pay the same in both modes and differ only graphically. Wild can stand for any other symbol, except Scatter, to make the best possible winning combination. There is also a separate payout for two or more Wild symbols on an active payline, as seen in the Paytable. This is paid out instead of the regular symbol win if the win amount from the Wild symbols is larger than the win from the regular symbols (by Wild standing in).

Three or more Wild symbols simultaneously in any position, not necessarily on a particular payline, during Jungle Mode, trigger the Wild Jungle Respins feature.

Three or more Wild symbols simultaneously in any position, not necessarily on a particular payline, during Big City Mode, trigger the Wild Kong Respins feature.

Wild Jungle Respins feature

If three or more Wild symbols appear in any position on the reels simultaneously, even off the paylines, during Jungle Mode, the Wild Jungle Respins feature is triggered.

The player is awarded 3 free respins, during which more Wild symbols are added to the reels and every time a Wild symbol appears, it remains frozen until the feature ends.

Click on Click to Start to start the feature.

During the free respins, the reels are spun automatically using the same number of lines and bet per line as on the spin that won the feature.

The Skull Island Bonus cannot be triggered during the Wild Jungle Respins feature. The Wild Jungle Respins feature cannot be retriggered during the respins.

If the Wild Jungle Respins feature is triggered during the Auto Play mode, the feature begins after the player clicks on Click to Start. When the feature spins are over, the player clicks on Continue to return to the regular gameplay.

Wild Kong Respins feature

If three or more Wild symbols appear in any position on the reels simultaneously, even off the paylines, during Big City Mode, the Wild Kong Respins feature is triggered.

The player is awarded 3 free respins. In the first respin, all symbols on reels #1 and #5 are Wild. In the second respin, all symbols on reels #2 and #4 are Wild. In the third respin, all symbols on reels #1, #3 and #5 are Wild. On the reels that are covered with Wild symbols, an animation is played of King Kong chasing a car in a city. The rest of the reels spin regularly. The Wild symbols substitute for all other symbols except Scatter.

During the free respins, the reels are spun automatically using the same number of lines and bet per line as on the spin that won the feature.

The City Tower Bonus cannot be triggered during the Wild Kong Respins feature. The Wild Kong Respins feature cannot be retriggered during the respins.

Click on Click to Start to start the feature.

If the Wild Kong Respins feature is triggered during the Auto Play mode, the feature begins after the player clicks on Click to Start. When the feature spins are over, the player clicks on Continue to return to the regular gameplay.

Scatter Symbol

The Scatter symbols do not have to occur on any particular payline.

If there are three or more Scatter symbols in any position on the reels in either mode, the payout earned is multiplied by the total bet and added to payline winnings.

Three or more Scatter symbols in any position on the reels simultaneously during Jungle Mode, trigger the Skull Island Bonus.

Three or more Scatter symbols in any position on the reels simultaneously during Big City Mode, trigger the City Tower Bonus.

Skull Island Bonus

If three or more Scatter symbols appear in any position on the reels simultaneously during Jungle Mode, the Skull Island Bonus is triggered.

To start the Skull Island Bonus round, click on Click to Start.
In the Skull Island Bonus, the player is presented a map with 13 locations. Click on a location to uncover one out of three creatures or King Kong. Play until you uncover three of the same kind of creature or until you have found one King Kong. Each creature is worth a different cash prize which is a multiple of the total bet of the spin that triggered the Skull Island Bonus (X10, X15 or X20 for creatures or X30 for King Kong). The maximum total amount that can be won from the Skull Island Bonus is 30 times the total bet of the spin that triggered the feature.

At the end of the Skull Island Bonus, a win screen displays your winnings. Click Continue to return to the regular slot reels, where normal gameplay resumes in Big City Mode.

If the Skull Island Bonus is triggered during the Auto Play mode, the feature begins after the player clicks on Click to Start. When the bonus round is over, the player clicks Continue on the screen summarizing the feature win and the Auto Play mode resumes in Big City Mode.

City Tower Bonus

If three or more Scatter symbols appear in any position on the reels simultaneously during Big City Mode, the City Tower Bonus is triggered.

To start the City Tower Bonus round, click on Click to Start.
In the City Tower Bonus, King Kong is on top of a high city tower, with planes flying around him. The player must pick 1 out of 3 planes on three separate occasions. Clicking on a selection wins a cash prize, which is a multiple of the total bet of the spin that triggered the City Tower Bonus (from X1 to X8) and begins a short clip in which King Kong takes down a plane. Once the clip is over, if there are any more selections to be made, the player is returned to the screen in which another plane must be picked. Taking down three planes ends the City Tower Bonus. The maximum total amount that can be won from the City Tower Bonus is 23 times the total bet of the spin that triggered the feature.

At the end of the City Tower Bonus, a win screen displays your winnings. Click Continue to return to the regular slot reels, where normal gameplay resumes in Jungle Mode.

If the City Tower Bonus is triggered during the Auto Play mode, the feature begins after the player clicks on Click to Start. When the bonus round is over, the player clicks Continue on the screen summarizing the feature win and the Auto Play mode resumes in Jungle Mode.
